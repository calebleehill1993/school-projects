About

My name is Elizabeth Hill and I love to create! I first really started getting into crafts when I was in high school and learned to knit and discovered dollar bill origami. Since then, I have continued to create using the plethora of ideas and resources available on the internet, that I often tweak and make my own. I am a total cheapskate, so my favorite projects are always the ones I can do with only the supplies I have at home, which often means some unusual inventions and unexpected recycling of random material.

I currently live in Provo with my incredibly patient and indulgent husband, and teach 6th grade at a local Elementary school.
